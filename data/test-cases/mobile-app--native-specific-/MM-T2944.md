## MM-T2944: (Tablet) Advanced - Ctrl-Enter (post text box)

---

**Step 1**

1. Go to Account Settings ➜ Advanced ➜ Send Messages on Ctrl + Enter ➜ Edit

3. Save

**On mobile**:

- Hitting Enter should create a new line.
